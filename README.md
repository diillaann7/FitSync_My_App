# FitSync – AI Powered Fitness Tracker

FitSync is a modern AI-powered fitness and nutrition tracking app designed to help users track workouts, monitor nutrition, analyze progress, and stay motivated through gamification and intelligent recommendations.

The app combines fitness tracking, nutrition planning, AI coaching, and social features into one powerful platform ready for App Store and Google Play deployment.

---

# Features

## Core Fitness Tracking

- Workout tracking and logging
- Exercise database
- Personal records tracking
- Weekly workout planner
- Body metrics tracking
  - weight
  - body fat
  - measurements
- Progress dashboard with analytics

---

## Nutrition & Macros

- Calorie tracking
- Macro tracking (protein, carbs, fats)
- Meal logging
- Nutrition logs
- Smart macro suggestions
